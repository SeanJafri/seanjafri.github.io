About

I'm a recent graduate from the University of Texas at Arlington (UTA) with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering, which is detailed in the attached resume. I have developed my abilities in 3D CAD design and modeling, a strong understanding of materials, dynamic simulations, and experience with different manufacturing methods.

The projects I've worked on have given me hands-on experience working with electromechanical systems, from project inception through engineering design and manufacture to the operation and maintenance of the system. Proven ability to coordinate technical teams, manage budgets, and approach complex problems with cost-effective solutions.
